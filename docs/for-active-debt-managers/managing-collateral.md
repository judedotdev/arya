# Managing Collateral

### What Is Collateral in DeFi?

Collateral is the asset you deposit to secure a loan. If the value of your collateral drops too low relative to your borrowed amount, your position may be **liquidated**.

With Arya, you can:

* Deposit multiple assets as collateral
* Adjust collateral at any time
* Monitor risk in real time
* Set up alerts or automate responses

***

### Key Metrics to Understand

| Term                      | Definition                                                           |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Loan-to-Value (LTV)**   | % of your collateral's value that you can borrow                     |
| **Liquidation Threshold** | Point at which your position becomes vulnerable to liquidation       |
| **Health Factor**         | Safety score of your position; above 1.0 is safe, below 1.0 is risky |

***

### How to Manage Collateral in Arya

**1. Add More Collateral**

* Go to the **Collateral Management** section
* Choose the asset you want to add
* Input amount and approve transaction
* Your borrowing power and health factor will update automatically

**2. Withdraw Collateral (If Safe)**

* If your health factor is comfortably high, you can withdraw part of your collateral
* Select the asset and withdrawal amount
* Arya will prevent withdrawals that would put your position at risk

**3. Replace or Switch Collateral**

**4. Monitor Risk in Real Time**

* Dashboard shows:
  * Current collateral value
  * Borrow balance
  * Real-time health factor
  * Market price feed for your collateral
* Enable notifications if health factor drops below your set threshold

### Automation Features

* **Auto-Top-Up :** Keep collateral levels healthy by automatically transferring from your wallet
* **Collateral Rebalancing:** Based on volatility or new yield opportunities, Arya can reallocate your collateral across protocols
* **Liquidation Protection:** Trigger automatic repayment or top-up if your position becomes risky
